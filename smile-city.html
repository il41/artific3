Smile City - 2024

We created a touchscreen installation at Explora for their Since of Kindness exhibition.


Smile City uses a 27" multitouch screen in a custom wooden housing running the Smile City simulation.

Museum visitors tap the denizens to seed an act of kindness and see how it spreads. Animated acts of kindness spread happiness and color among the denizens.
